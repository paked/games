paked's games

i try to make games. i'm interested in design which relaxes, provides agency, and respects players. i usually prefer to work without an engine (but don't bash those who do), as it's where I feel most at ease. i design, code, pixel, and sometimes make sounds or music.

if you're looking for my non-game related work check out harrison.tech.

this is a collection of the various things I've worked on over time (be they prototypes or not). it's not a conclusive list, i've only included my REALLY old projects if i was particularly proud of them. i'm going to try write a little post-mortem for each thing at some point.

  • 3 Years of Echo Mountain

    a prototype i initially wanted to complete for Ludum Dare, which ended up taking an extra 2 months due to scope screep. based around mechanics tied together by "electricity" which each mechanic used in it's own way. i killed it as it's scope continued to grow and i realised it'd never be done.

    harrison.tech/basin/game.html | Source (github.com/paked/basin) | April 2018 — June 2018
  • P U L S E

    a super small prototype i made about cells, virality, and cutting things out. aims to be instructionless, kind of fails at it.

    harrison.tech/pulse | Source (github.com/paked/basin) | February 2018
  • VOXEL THING

    a Minecraft style voxel "game" with an "editor" save-format and VR support. my first time playing around with 3D programming. built with C++, OpenGL, GLFW, and cmake. using learnopengl.com as my learning resource.

    Source (github.com/paked/voxel-thing) | October 2017 — December 2017
  • INFINTE STATE MACHINIMA

    inspired by the design of Jonathan Blow in The Witness and Seymour Papert's writings on learning i set out to try creating my own project which taught a "visual language". the goal was to create an experience which with no instructions someone could play from start to finish.

    harrison.tech/infinite-state-machinima | Source | March 2017
